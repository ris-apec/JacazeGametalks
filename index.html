About Gaming

Gaming is a game or entertainment mostly played by people/gamers to entertain themselves; video games are popular with gamers. Games such as Valorant, Mobile Legends Bang Bang, Minecraft, Roblox, Call of Duty, League of Legends, and more are popular games commonly played by gamers; they use electronic devices, such as computers, mobile phones, tablets, and others.

Quick thinking!

Gaming is fun and stress-free. It improves critical thinking, hand-eye coordination, and even problem-solving skills. Conversely, excessive gaming leads to addiction, health complications, and negatives in everyday life.

Benefits of gaming

Gaming improves the cognitive abilities, reduces stress, and develops teamwork but excessive gaming may result in addiction as well as decreased physical activities that contribute to a lesser level of well-being.


Impact Of Gaming

Gaming improves children's ability to solve problems, increase their creativity, and enhance teamwork but also promotes social contact and learning. However, excessive gaming will lead to less physical activity, and there is a likelihood of behavioral problems.
